About

While working for numerous cleaning companies, Patty often felt like she could provide better services than with those under which she was employed. Her dedication to customer satisfaction and her drive to exceed industry standards naturally led her to begin her own cleaning business. She decided to make her dream a reality by establishing Verde Cleaning in 2015. Patty leads her company employing best practices in cleaning, as well as integrity and honesty in the company’s processes and customer service. She has a genuine interest in making sure that the customer is 100% satisfied. When her customers are happy, she is happy. She feels her work is valued when her cleanings enable her customers to step away from cleaning their own home or small business so they can take on life’s challenges and joys.

"We're proud to provide our employees with exceptional training, a fair wage and managable work schedule so they can be successful at providing you with your cleaning needs. Customers may choose a green clean option, which is a cleaner option for our employees and your home and work environment." -Patty
